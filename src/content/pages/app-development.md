**An app is not a project with an end date – it is a product with a life story.**

We develop mobile apps for iOS and Android and accompany them through their entire lifecycle: from the first requirements through development and app store deployment to updates in year ten. Since 2006, for digital agencies, SMEs and corporations.

## What's included

- **Requirements engineering** – Understanding what is really needed: analyzing, weighing, defining and documenting. This is where app projects succeed or fail – which is why every one of our projects starts here.
- **Conception and prototyping** – Personas, user journeys, wireframes and clickable prototypes make the app idea concrete before expensive code is written.
- **UX/UI implementation** – We work closely with your designers, agencies and marketing owners.
- **Development** – Cross-platform with [Ionic/Capacitor, React Native or Flutter](/en/technologies/), native where it earns its keep. Well-structured, high-performance, documented code.
- **API and backend integration** – Interfaces in the app, on the server side and to third-party systems such as CMS, shops or internal IT.
- **Quality assurance** – Coding guidelines, peer reviews, unit and end-to-end tests.
- **App store deployment** – Certificates, provisioning, review processes: we bring the app into the stores – and know the pitfalls from countless review rounds.

## How we work together

Three models, depending on who you are:

- **[For digital agencies](/en/apps-for-digital-agencies/)** – white-label development in the background; you own the client relationship.
- **[For SMEs and start-ups](/en/apps-for-smes-and-startups/)** – the complete package from idea to store, including knowledge transfer to your team.
- **[For enterprise IT](/en/enterprise-mobile-apps/)** – we work inside your teams, with your workflows, tools and compliance processes.

After launch, our [app maintenance](/en/app-maintenance/) takes over on request – so the app is still current, secure and in the store five years from now.

## Frequently asked questions

### What does an app cost?

Honest answer: it depends on scope – a companion app with a few features is a different animal than a customer portal with backend integration. After a first conversation we can narrow the effort down seriously. More important than the initial price: the cost over the app's whole lifetime. Cross-platform development and automation are what keep exactly that low.

### How long does development take?

From kick-off to store release, typically three to nine months depending on scope – including requirements and app review. We produce a reliable timeline after the requirements analysis.

### Cross-platform or native?

For most requirements cross-platform is the more economical choice: one code base, both platforms. Where native functionality is needed we integrate native modules. How we choose is described on our [technology page](/en/technologies/).

### Who owns the code?

You do. We work source-available: you get full access to the source code and repositories – no vendor lock-in.

### What happens after launch?

OS updates, new devices, store policies, security patches: an app needs continuous care. That is what our [app maintenance](/en/app-maintenance/) with fixed monthly packages is for.
