**The best app is worthless if nobody finds it.**

App store optimization (ASO) is to apps what SEO is to websites – and just as often underestimated. We make sure your app gets found, installed and well rated in the stores. As a team of developers and digital marketers we do this from one source: whoever built the app optimizes its presence more credibly.

## What's included

- **ASO audit** – Where does your app stand today? Keywords, conversion, ratings, competition: an honest assessment with prioritized actions.
- **Store presence** – Title, subtitle, keywords, descriptions, screenshots and preview videos – for App Store and Google Play, in German and English.
- **Review and rating management** – Responding to reviews, feeding feedback back into development, timing rating prompts correctly.
- **Launch support** – Staged rollouts, release notes people actually read, and a store presence that is ready at launch – not after it.
- **Continuous optimization** – ASO is not a one-off project: watching rankings and conversion, sharpening, reporting. Included in the [Care+Grow maintenance package](/en/app-maintenance/).

## Frequently asked questions

### What exactly is ASO?

Optimizing your store presence so the app appears for relevant searches (visibility) and visitors actually install it (conversion). Together these two decide organic growth.

### Does it work without an advertising budget?

Yes – ASO works on organic discoverability. Paid campaigns can complement it, but they are no substitute for a good store presence: paid traffic also lands on your store page and has to be convinced there.

### When should ASO start?

Before launch. Name, keywords and store presence can be changed later – but the first impression and the first ratings shape the ranking lastingly.

### How do we measure success?

Keyword rankings, store impressions, conversion rate and install numbers – metrics the stores themselves provide and that we report regularly.
