## Why a subscription instead of one-off jobs?

- **Predictability** – fixed costs instead of surprise invoices whenever Apple or Google change something.
- **Speed** – we know your code base and pipeline; critical fixes don't need to become a project first.
- **Value retention** – an app maintained continuously costs far less over five years than two "rescue projects".

## Frequently asked questions

### Do you take over apps that someone else built?

Yes, we do that regularly. It starts with a code audit: we assess the code base, dependencies and build process and tell you honestly what shape the app is in.

### What counts as maintenance, what as a new feature?

Maintenance keeps the app running, secure and store-compliant. New functionality is further development – the Care+Grow package includes a monthly budget for it; larger initiatives are planned separately.

### How fast do you react to critical problems?

Response times are agreed individually in the maintenance contract – matched to how business-critical your app is.

### What happens when a major OS release comes out?

We test your app against the iOS and Android beta versions before the release reaches your users – and fix problems before they show up in your store rating.
