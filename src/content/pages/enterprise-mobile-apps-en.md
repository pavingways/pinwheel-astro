**Full-service partner for enterprise apps and services.**

As part of IT departments in global corporations we go far beyond just programming apps. Our years of experience from numerous projects allow us to offer the entire app development chain in high quality.

## Our enterprise services

- **Integration into your processes** – We work in your teams, with your workflows and tools, and embed app development into your existing IT landscape.
- **API and interface development** – We specify and implement APIs in the app, on the server side and to third-party systems.
- **Pipeline setup and automation** – Git-based workflows, continuous integration and delivery, GitHub Actions and end-to-end tests are part of our everyday work.
- **Quality assurance** – Coding guidelines, peer reviews, unit and end-to-end tests safeguard quality across the entire lifecycle.
- **App store deployment** – Certificates, private keys, provisioning profiles and review processes: we take care of the way into the stores.
- **Workshops and knowledge transfer** – We pass on knowledge and experience, hands-on in your team or as half-day and full-day workshops.

Discretion, reliability and long-term collaboration go without saying.
