**The right technology is the one that fits your project.**

We have been developing mobile apps for iOS and Android since 2006 – long enough not to fight religious wars over frameworks. Our technology recommendation follows your requirements, your team and your budget, not trends.

## Cross-platform first

For most projects we rely on cross-platform development: one code base for both platforms, faster iterations and lower costs over the entire lifetime of the app.

- **Ionic / Capacitor** – apps built on web technologies: ideal when web know-how already exists in the team or when web and app versions should share one code base.
- **React Native** – native UI with React: proven for apps with high demands on look & feel in a React-savvy environment.
- **Flutter** – Google's UI toolkit: strong for individual designs and consistent behavior on both platforms.

Where native functionality is required – from push notifications to camera and sensors to platform-specific APIs – we develop native modules and integrate them cleanly.

## Automation: CI/CD and testing

We automate builds, signing and app store deployment with **fastlane** and **GitHub Actions**. Unit tests and end-to-end tests safeguard quality across every version – from the first release to the update in year ten.

## APIs and backends

Apps live on their data. We specify and implement interfaces – in the app, on the server side and to third-party systems such as CMS, shops or internal IT landscapes.

## Which technology fits your project?

The fastest way to find out is a no-obligation conversation – with an honest look at requirements, team and budget.
